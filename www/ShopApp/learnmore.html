About Grouper

Shopping made easy.

Grouper is a service provided for UCF students without no reliable transportation or time to shop.Search for a convenient compatible shopper to shop for your groceries. Whether you have no car, busy studying, working out at the gym, or taking a nap, Grouper connects students and opens the possibility to have your favorite snacks, goods or essential everyday to day items delivered right to your door step, without ever setting a foot outside your home.
